Our integrated **AI Chatbot** is designed to provide real-time assistance and quick answers to your questions while navigating the Gateway documentation. Powered by advanced AI technology, this tool helps you find relevant information, troubleshooting tips, and usage instructions without having to search manually.

### **Why Use the AI Chatbot?**

* **Instant Answers**: Receive quick responses to common questions, helping you save time and stay focused.

* **Search Documentation**: The chatbot is directly integrated with our documentation, making it easy to pull up guides, API references, and troubleshooting articles.

* **24/7 Assistance**: Access the AI chatbot anytime for around-the-clock support, without waiting for a support representative.

* **Troubleshooting Help**: Get suggestions for resolving common issues as you work through different aspects of the Gateway.

### **How to Access the AI Chatbot**

To start using the AI chatbot:

1. **Use the Search Bar**: At the top of any page in our documentation, enter your question or keyword into the search bar.

2. **Instant Suggestions**: As you type, the AI chatbot will provide suggested articles and answers, helping you find relevant information faster.

3. **Explore Results**: Click on a suggestion or press **Enter** to view detailed responses and related documentation that address your query.

<Note>
  The AI Chatbot is designed for informational and troubleshooting assistance. For more complex issues or account-specific help, please [contact our support team](./support/contact).
</Note>

## **Tips for Effective Use**

* **Be Specific**: For the best results, ask specific questions (e.g., "How do I set up a Subscription product?" rather than "Setup help").

* **Explore Suggestions**: The chatbot often provides links to relevant documentation. Click these links to explore the full guides.

* **Try Multiple Queries**: If the first response isn’t helpful, try rephrasing your question or breaking it into smaller parts.
