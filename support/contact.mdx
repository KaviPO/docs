Our support team is here to help with any questions or issues you may encounter while using the Gateway. Whether you need technical assistance, have account-related inquiries, or want guidance on using specific features, we’re here to assist you.

## **When to Contact Support**

<Accordion title="Account Setup & Onboarding">
  If you’re new to the Gateway, we recommend reviewing the [Onboarding Checklist](/documentation/checklist) first. If you still have questions, contact us for personalized help.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Technical Issues & Troubleshooting">
  For API errors, payment processing issues, or plugin installation problems, please reach out to our support team. You can also check our [FAQs & Troubleshooting](./faqs) for quick solutions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Billing & Account Management">
  For inquiries related to billing, account upgrades, or changes, contact support, and we’ll guide you through the necessary steps.
</Accordion>
