## **Implementation Notes**

* **Currency Selection**: Ensure that your integration allows customers to select their preferred currency during the checkout process.

* **Currency Conversion**: Be aware of any conversion fees that may apply if the transaction involves currency conversion.

* **Compliance**: Ensure compliance with local regulations regarding currency transactions in each market.

<Note>
  Currency conversion fees may vary by region and payment method. Be sure to check for any specific requirements or fees for each currency.
</Note>
