## **How Pay by Link Works**

**Generate Link:** Add items in the card and create a unique payment link in the gateway with transaction details like amount and currency.

**Share with Customers:** Send the link via email, SMS, or messaging platforms.

**Secure Payment:** Customers complete the transaction on the HPP page.

**Payment Invoice:** Send payment completion invoice to the cardholder after successful completion of the payment.

## **Getting Started with Pay by Link**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate the Payment Link">
    * Merchants log into the payment gateway and create a unique payment link.

    * Customize the link with transaction details such as:

      * Enter Payment amount.

      * Select currency.

      * Mention expiration date or validity period.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share the Link with Customers">
    The generated payment link is shared with customers through various channels, including:

    * Email: Attach the link with invoices or messages.

    * SMS: Send directly to the customers phone.

    * Chat Platforms: Share on WhatsApp, social media messengers, or other communication tools.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customer Accesses the Link">
    Customers click the link, which redirects them to Hosted Payment Page (HPP).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customer Completes the Payment">
    * On the payment page, card holder enter their payment details (e.g., card information or digital wallet credentials).

    * Customer Completes the Payment

    * Payments are processed securely in real time.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Transaction Confirmation">
    * Once the payment is processed, both the merchant and the customer receive a confirmation.

    * Merchants can track the status of the payment through their gateway dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Customize each link with a link name, an expiry date and item details for a better user experience.</Tip>

## **How Customers Use Pay by Link**
<Steps>
  
  <Step title="Receive the Payment Link">
     - Customers receive a secure payment link shared by the merchant via email, SMS, or chat platforms like WhatsApp.  
   - The link includes transaction details such as the amount, due date, and purpose of payment. 
  </Step>
  
 <Step title="Click the Link">
    - Customers click on the shared link, which redirects them to a hosted payment page.  
  </Step>

<Step title="Review Transaction Details">
    - On the payment page, customers can review the transaction amount and other details to ensure accuracy.
  </Step>

<Step title="Enter Payment Information">
    - Customers input their payment details, such as:  
     - Credit or debit card information.  
     - Digital wallet credentials.  
     - Address
     - Valid Email 
  </Step>

<Step title="Complete the Payment">
   - Once the information is entered, customers confirm and submit the payment.  
   - The payment is processed securely in real time.  

  </Step>

<Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    - Upon successful payment, customers receive a confirmation message  and payment completion invoice on the provided email.
  </Step>
  
</Steps>
